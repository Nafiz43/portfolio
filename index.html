Grad Student | Researcher | Tech Enthusiast

Hi! I am pursuing PhD in Computer Science at University of California - Davis, where I work in DECAL lab with professor Vladimir Filkov. Formerly, I served as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at MIST. My research interests broadly lies in:

  • application of large language models (LLMs), machine learning (ML), and deep learning (DL) in software engineering and health informatics

  • building efficient and scalable ML/DL pipelines for real-world problem solving

  • fine-tuning large language models for domain-specific applications

  • developing retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) frameworks

Apart from my academic activities, I like to explore new things and embrace unknown challenges in my life. In my spare time, I love playing table tennis and badminton.
